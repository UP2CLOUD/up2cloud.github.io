Privacy at a Glance

Short version: UP2CLOUD only collects data necessary to respond to enquiries, improve user experience, and operate core website functionality. Personal data is never sold, rented, or used for advertising targeting.

What We Collect

Contact form details, optional business information, anonymised analytics, and chatbot conversation prompts submitted voluntarily.

What We Never Do

No advertising cookies, no third-party data sales, no behavioral profiling, and no payment information processing.

Who We Are

UP2CLOUD is a Portugal-based cloud engineering and infrastructure consulting company led by Cesar A. Nogueira, operating from Vila Real.

Throughout this policy, “UP2CLOUD”, “we”, “our”, and “us” refer to the UP2CLOUD consulting business and website operations available through up2cloud.tech.

Contact: hello@up2cloud.io · +351 937 471 554

Data We Collect

Contact Forms

  • Full name
  • Work email address
  • Company name (optional)
  • Requested service area (optional)
  • Message content submitted voluntarily

Form submissions are processed through FormSubmit and delivered securely to the UP2CLOUD inbox. Form data is not stored on dedicated UP2CLOUD servers.

Analytics & UX Monitoring

UP2CLOUD uses Hotjar to understand how visitors interact with the website and identify UX improvements.

  • Anonymous click and scroll interactions
  • Session behavior analysis
  • Approximate location at city/country level
  • Browser and device type

AI Chat Assistant

Chat prompts entered into the AI assistant are securely transmitted to Groq infrastructure for processing using Llama models. Messages are transmitted over HTTPS.

Legal Basis for Processing

For users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), UP2CLOUD processes data under the following GDPR legal bases:

  • Legitimate Interest — responding to direct business enquiries initiated by users.
  • Consent — analytics and optional interaction monitoring.
  • Contractual Necessity — where data processing becomes necessary to provide requested consulting services.

How We Use Your Data

  • Responding to consulting enquiries and support requests
  • Improving website usability and UX performance
  • Monitoring infrastructure reliability and security
  • Preventing abuse, spam, or malicious usage

UP2CLOUD does not send unsolicited marketing communications without explicit consent.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

Data may only be shared with carefully selected infrastructure and operational providers necessary to operate the platform:

  • FormSubmit — secure form delivery
  • Hotjar — anonymised UX analytics
  • Groq — AI inference processing
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting infrastructure

UP2CLOUD does not sell or commercialize personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

Data Retention

Business correspondence and submitted enquiries may be retained for up to 24 months to maintain communication history and consulting continuity.

Users may request deletion of personal data at any time by contacting: hello@up2cloud.io

Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, users may request:

  • Access to personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Restriction or objection to processing
  • Export of personal data in machine-readable format

Requests are typically answered within 30 days.

Cookies & Analytics

UP2CLOUD uses minimal cookies and browser storage technologies strictly for user experience and analytics functionality.

  • Language preference storage
  • Hotjar analytics cookies
  • Session continuity features

No advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or retargeting systems are used.

Security & Infrastructure

All website traffic is encrypted using HTTPS with TLS 1.3. Infrastructure components follow cloud security best practices, including strict HTTPS enforcement and minimized attack surface principles.

UP2CLOUD does not intentionally collect sensitive financial or health information through the public website.

Contact & Supervisory Authority

Privacy-related questions may be sent directly to: hello@up2cloud.io

Users located in Portugal may also contact the CNPD (Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados): www.cnpd.pt
